Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 21, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Skillful handles information when you use the application. Skillful is an offline productivity application designed to help users track skills, goals, practice sessions, progress, and achievements.

2. Information You Enter

You may voluntarily enter information such as skill names, categories, descriptions, practice duration, ratings, notes, goals, and progress values.

This information is provided directly by you and is used only to provide the application's features.

3. Local Data Storage

The current version of Skillful stores application data locally on your device using local application storage. Your records are not uploaded to developer-operated cloud servers.

4. No Account Required

Skillful does not require account registration, login credentials, social sign-in, or identity verification.

5. Data Collection

The current version of Skillful does not collect, sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties.

6. Advertising and Analytics

The current version does not contain third-party advertising, advertising identifiers, behavioral tracking, or developer-operated analytics services.

7. Permissions

Skillful does not require sensitive device permissions to provide its main skill-tracking functionality.

If future versions introduce an optional feature that requires a device permission, the permission will be requested through the operating system and the related privacy information will be updated.

8. Data Retention

Your information remains stored on your device until you delete individual records, reset the application data, clear the application's storage, or uninstall Skillful.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete information from inside the application. You can also remove locally stored information by using the reset option in Settings, clearing the application's storage through your device settings, or uninstalling the application.

10. Data Security

Because data is stored locally, the security of your information depends partly on your device security. We recommend using a screen lock, keeping your operating system updated, and preventing unauthorized access to your device.

11. Data Backup

The current version does not provide developer-operated cloud backup or synchronization. You are responsible for maintaining any separate backup you consider necessary.

12. Children's Privacy

Skillful is a general productivity application and is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. The application does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Third-Party Services

The current version does not depend on third-party login, cloud storage, advertising, or analytics services. Distribution platforms such as Google Play may process information independently under their own policies.

14. International Data Transfers

Since the current version does not upload your application records to developer-operated servers, Skillful does not perform developer-controlled international transfers of those records.

15. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when application features or privacy practices change. The updated version will display a revised “Last updated” date.
